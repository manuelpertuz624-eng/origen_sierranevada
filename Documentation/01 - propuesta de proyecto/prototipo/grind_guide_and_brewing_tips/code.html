The Ritual

V60 Preparation

"Precision and patience are the keys to unlocking the floral notes of Sierra Nevada beans."

Dose 20g
Water 300ml
Temp 93°C
Total Time 3:00
1

Rinse & Warm

Fold the filter paper and place it in the dripper. Rinse thoroughly with hot water to remove any paper taste and to warm your vessel. Discard the water.

2

The Bloom

Add your 20g of medium-fine ground coffee. Pour 40-50g of water gently over the grounds. Watch them bubble and rise. Wait 30-45 seconds. This releases CO2.

3

Steady Pour

Starting from the center, pour in a steady spiral motion towards the edge and back. Aim to reach 300g total weight by the 2:00 mark. Keep the water level consistent.

4

Draw Down & Serve

Allow the water to drip through completely. The bed of coffee grounds should look flat. Give the carafe a swirl to aerate and mix, then serve immediately.
