The Story Behind Each Bean

“

High in the misty peaks of the Sierra Nevada, where the clouds touch the soil, lies the origin of Café Malu. Cultivated by hands that have known these lands for centuries, our beans are shade-grown under a canopy of native trees, preserving the biodiversity of the rainforest.

Every cherry is hand-picked at peak ripeness, fermented for 24 hours, and sun-dried on raised beds. This meticulous process unlocks the unique flavor profile that earned a score of 88 points from the Specialty Coffee Association.
